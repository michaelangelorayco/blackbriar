A. Create an IAM group.



A1.From the AWS management console, navigate to IAM.





You will be redirected to the IAM Dashboard:







A2.Let us create an IAM group by clicking on Groups, then Create New Group.



Set the Group Name to consultant followed by the cadet id assigned to you separated by a dash, then click next.

It is also important to take note of your group name, we will ask for it before you end the lab.




A3.Attach the policy "ReadOnlyAccess" and "IAMUserChangePassword".



Select ReadOnlyAccess.



Select IAMUserChangePassword then click next step.





A4.Before creating the group, please make sure to take note of the policies, we will ask for it before you end the lab.



Click on create group, you now have created an IAM Group.





A5.View the details of the IAM Group and it's very important to take note of the Group ARN. We will ask for it before you end the lab.
