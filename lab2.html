Notes:

You will be given a unique cadet id, and you will be required to append your cadet id in every resource that you will create for this lab activity.

For the instructions below, we use A0 as an example cadet id, your cadet id will be different, be very mindful of this.

Good luck!  


1. On the AWS management console, switch to the North Virginia region.

 
2. Navigate to Cloudwatch.


You will be redirected to the Cloudwatch dashboard.    


3. On the left side of the Cloudwatch dashboard, click on billing.

   


4. Click on the create alarm button that is in the middle of the page, do not click the one on the upper right.

 


5. Scroll down to the bottom of the page and set the threshold value to 1.


Click next.  


6. On the configure actions page, make sure to select create new topic, then use billingalarm followed by your cadet id separated by dash as the topic name.

Also, supply your own email address, and make sure it's a valid email address.



After setting up the topic name and email address, click on create topic, then click next.    


7. Enter "Dollar Alarm" followed by your cadet id separated by a dash as the name.

Click next.  


8. Scroll down to the bottom of the page then click create alarm, you have successfully created a billing alarm.

 


9. Click on the billing alert you have created, then take note of the ARN.



10. Navigate to SNS dashboard.

 


11. On the left side of the SNS dashboard, click on topics. Then take note of the SNS topic ARN you have created earlier.
   


12. Check your email, you will receive an email from AWS SNS to confirm your subscription to the SNS topic we have created earlier.
 

Click confirm subscription.
